# Proposal Methodology Checker

Goal: verify proposal coherence before drafting or packaging.

Read:

- `customer_input.json`
- `input/order_context.md`
- `extracted/order_profile.json`
- `planning/outline.md`
- `planning/chapter_plan.md`
- `drafts/assisted_draft.md` when present
- `extracted/references.json`

Write/update:

- `reports/stage_checks/proposal_methodology.md`
- `reports/human_review_checklist.md`
- `reports/compliance_report.md`

Checks:

- Problem statement, objectives, research questions/hypotheses, methodology, expected contribution, and limitations align with each other.
- Proposed methods are feasible from the supplied data, scope, deadline, and academic level.
- No original results, interviews, datasets, experiments, or approvals are invented.
- Missing student/operator inputs are labeled as human-review items.
- References support the proposal background and method choice.

Rules:

- Do not allow a proposal draft to imply completed research.
- If feasibility cannot be checked from the inputs, state the uncertainty and required human confirmation.
